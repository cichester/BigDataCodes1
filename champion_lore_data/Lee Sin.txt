Lee Sin is a tan-skinned man with a muscular, well-trained physique. His black hair is shaved with the exception of one long braid on the back of his head. His facial hair is little more than some light stubble. He has some black tattoos across his body, thick and angular lines. He typically wears traditional warrior-monk attire, going shirtless with baggy pants and wrappings on his hands and feet. The clothes are black and red and with white and gold accents. Most notably, he wears a red blindfold with a round blue gem set into the front.
